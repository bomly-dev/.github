# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make our community welcoming, safe, and equitable for all.

We are committed to fostering an environment that respects and promotes the
dignity, rights, and contributions of all individuals, regardless of
characteristics including race, ethnicity, caste, color, age, physical
characteristics, neurodiversity, disability, sex or gender, gender identity or
expression, sexual orientation, language, philosophy or religion, national or
social origin, socio-economic position, level of education, or other status. The
same privileges of participation are extended to everyone who participates in
good faith and in accordance with this Covenant.

## Encouraged Behaviors

We strive to meet our community's expectations for positive behavior. We also
understand that words and actions may be interpreted differently than intended
based on culture, background, or native language.

With these considerations in mind, we agree to:

1. Respect the purpose of our community, our activities, and our ways of
   gathering.
2. Engage kindly and honestly with others.
3. Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
4. Take responsibility for our actions and contributions.
5. Gracefully give and accept constructive feedback.
6. Commit to repairing harm when it occurs.
7. Behave in other ways that promote and sustain the well-being of our
   community.

## Restricted Behaviors

Instances, threats, and promotion of these behaviors are violations of this Code
of Conduct:

1. Harassment, including violating explicitly expressed boundaries or engaging
   in unnecessary personal attention after a clear request to stop.
2. Character attacks, including insulting, demeaning, or pejorative comments
   directed at a community member or group.
3. Stereotyping or discrimination on the basis of immutable identities or
   traits.
4. Sexualization that would generally be considered inappropriately intimate in
   the context or purpose of the community.
5. Violating confidentiality by sharing or acting on someone's personal or
   private information without permission.
6. Endangerment, including causing, encouraging, or threatening violence or
   other harm toward any person or group.
7. Other behavior that threatens the well-being of our community.

Other restricted behavior includes misleading identity, failing to credit
sources, sharing promotional material outside community norms, or irresponsibly
presenting content that includes, links to, or describes restricted behavior.

## Reporting an Issue

Tensions can occur between community members even when everyone is trying their
best to collaborate. Not every conflict is a code of conduct violation, but this
Code of Conduct reinforces norms that help avoid conflicts and minimize harm.

To report a possible violation, email the maintainers at <contact@bomly.dev>.
Reports are handled confidentially.

Community moderators take reports seriously and will make every effort to
respond in a timely manner. They will investigate reports of code of conduct
violations while prioritizing safety and confidentiality. Enforcement actions
are usually carried out in private with the involved parties, though
communicating to the broader community may be part of a mutually agreed
resolution.

## Addressing and Repairing Harm

If an investigation finds that this Code of Conduct has been violated, the
following enforcement ladder may be used based on the incident's impact. Lower
rungs may be skipped for severe violations.

1. Warning: a private written warning with repair steps such as apology,
   acknowledgement of responsibility, or clarification of expectations.
2. Temporarily limited activities: a time-limited cooldown period or limited
   access to particular channels or interactions.
3. Temporary suspension: a private written warning with conditions for return.
4. Permanent ban: access to community spaces, tools, and communication channels
   is removed.

This enforcement ladder is a guideline. It does not limit moderators' discretion
and judgment in keeping with the best interests of the community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces and also applies when
an individual is officially representing the community in public or private
spaces.

Examples of representing our community include using an official email address,
posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed
representative at an online or offline event.
